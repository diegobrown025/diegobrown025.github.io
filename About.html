About Me

Hello! My Name is Diego Brown.


I am a Los Angeles and Boston based afrolatino Producer, DJ, beginner software worker, and artist, dedicated to bringing technology and music together in harmony.



I currently attend Berklee College of Music and I am going to begin my senior year in the fall, I major in Electronic Production and Design and by trade I am a producer and pianist.



My roots and my heart are what I follow most when it comes to music. Growing up on the west coast and being multiple ethnicities shaped the sounds that I love to put forth and kept me grounded when needed.

Through the art of sampling, artists like Daft Punk, Logistics, and Jdilla meet the sounds of Flying Lotus and Andres Landero, creating vibrant pieces that are full of energy and groove.
